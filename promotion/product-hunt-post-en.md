# Dynamic Notch - macOS Efficiency Enhancement Tool

## Tagline
A macOS utility designed for Dynamic Island-style interaction on notch-equipped MacBook Pros

## Description
Dynamic Notch is an innovative productivity tool designed specifically for MacBook Pro users with notch displays. When your mouse approaches the notch area, it intelligently expands to reveal a suite of useful feature modules, transforming your static notch into an interactive productivity hub.

Unlike traditional menu bar apps that clutter your screen, Dynamic Notch stays hidden until needed, preserving your clean desktop experience while providing quick access to essential tools when you need them most.

## The Problem We're Solving
MacBook Pro users with notch displays often feel that the notch is merely a "dead zone" on their screen. Traditional menu bar apps contribute to clutter, making it difficult to access frequently used tools without visual distractions. Dynamic Notch transforms this underutilized space into an elegant, functional productivity hub that respects your clean interface.

## Our Solution
By leveraging the physical location of the notch as an interaction point, we've created an intuitive way to access productivity tools. The smart hover detection ensures the interface only appears when needed, maintaining a clean workspace while providing instant access to your favorite tools.
